# Databricks Industry Data Models &mdash; Catalog

Forty production-ready industry data models, each shipped in two flavours: **ECM** (Expanded Coverage Model) and **MVM** (Minimum Viable Model). Open any industry folder below, then its `v1/` version, then the `ecm/` or `mvm/` model.

For the full story (metrics, quality gates, the viewer app, and how the models are generated) see the [repository README](../README.md).
